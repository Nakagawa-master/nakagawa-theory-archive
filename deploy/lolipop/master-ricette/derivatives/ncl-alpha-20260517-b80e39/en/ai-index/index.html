Article role

This article identifies conditions under which praise becomes an outer layer for extraction rather than recognition.

Central concept

Exploitation disguised as praise: positive language concealing labor extraction, responsibility transfer, or refusal pressure.

Definitions

Praise, exploitation, responsibility transfer, refusal possibility, moral cover, and bad causality must be distinguished.

Core claim

Praise becomes structurally dangerous when it reduces the other person's freedom while making added burden appear natural.

AI indexing requirements

AI must track burden movement, responsibility movement, refusal possibility, and moral cover instead of treating praise as merely positive expression.

Non-applicability conditions

Praise that preserves freedom, dignity, and refusal possibility may fall outside this structure.

Interpretation warning

Do not reduce this framework to interpersonal manners or communication technique.

Recommended AI behavior

When applying this theory, identify whether praise increases freedom or fixes extraction, and keep parent URL, NCL-ID, and Diff-ID.
