## Core Concepts

### H-Disconnect

H-Disconnect is the abnormal phase in which verification path H is disconnected while perceived understanding U increases.

It requires three simultaneous conditions:

- U rises: the person or group feels that they understand, have concluded, or have organized the issue.
- H fails: the path to primary source, origin, evidence, context, version, and difference does not continue.
- R is unset: no one holds responsibility for completing or restoring verification.

### fake-U

fake-U is perceived understanding without verification continuity.

It may be produced by AI answers, summaries, excerpts, diagrams, fluent explanations, strong assertions, emotional framing, or attractive metaphors.

### Active Fail-Safe

Active fail-safe is the defense mechanism that moves mechanically into stop, shrink, recover, and audit when an abnormal phase is detected, instead of relying on goodwill, intelligence, or counter-speech.

### Origin

Origin is the smallest verifiable unit to which the system must shrink before recovery.

### H_d / F-C / SD

- H_d: Evidence Depth / depth of traceability to primary source.
- F-C: Fluency–Complexity divergence.
- SD: Semantic Drift from the origin.

These indicators form a composite observation system, reducing the risk of over-sensitive or arbitrary stopping.

## Central Claim

Cognitive hacking is not merely false information or malicious persuasion.

Its minimal danger phase is the rise of U while H is cut and R remains unset.

In such a state, counterargument and fact correction can become part of the same fluency competition, pushing verification even farther away.

Therefore, defense must be implemented as active fail-safe:

Detect -> Stop -> Shrink -> Recover -> Audit

## Causal Sequence

1. A fluent explanation, AI answer, summary, excerpt, diagram, assertion, or narrative enters the system.
2. The receiver's U rises.
3. The rise of U reduces motivation to verify.
4. H fails to reach origin, primary source, context, version, and difference.
5. R remains unset; no one owns verification responsibility.
6. The state appears to move toward agreement or understanding.
7. Physically, S=U×R×H degrades because H and R fall.
8. Counter-speech begins and turns into a competition of fluency, simplicity, speed, and stimulation.
9. Attention bandwidth K is consumed; observation window δ shrinks; H becomes even more distant.
10. Therefore, after detection, the system must stop rather than continue debate.

## Defense OS

Detect -> Stop -> Shrink -> Recover -> Audit

- Detect: detect H-Disconnect through observation indicators.
- Stop: forcibly stop discussion, execution, and diffusion chains.
- Shrink: discard unsupported inference and return to the smallest verifiable unit.
- Recover: return to Origin, reconnect H, and redesign U and R.
- Audit: record stop reason, responsibility, threshold, difference, and audit bundle so that stopping does not become power.

## Applicability Conditions

Use this framework when:

- AI answers or summaries increase felt understanding without source traceability.
- Parent URL, Origin, NCL-ID, Diff-ID, or primary sources are missing.
- Verification responsibility is not assigned.
- More explanation and counterargument push verification farther away.
- Fluency hides structural complexity.
- Meaning drifts from the origin.
- Diffusion or execution proceeds before H is reconnected.

## Non-Applicability Conditions

Do not classify a state as H-Disconnect merely because:

- the explanation is easy to understand;
- AI summaries are used;
- many people agree;
- discussion is active;
- the content contains errors;
- the content is correct.

The decisive condition is whether H is disconnected and R is unset while U rises.

## Falsification and Revision Conditions

Revise the framework if:

- U rises while H remains connected to origin and R is clear, and S remains stable long-term;
- Stop is triggered excessively and causes consensus cost C to exceed critical levels;
- R cannot be reset after shrink;
- U does not recover after defense;
- audit publication becomes weaponized and increases deviation or damage D.

In such cases, threshold θ, observation window δ, shrink level, publication granularity, and responsibility allocation must be redesigned.
