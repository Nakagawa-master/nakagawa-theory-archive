## Central Claim

Effort has not become unnecessary.

But effort can no longer be grounded only in endurance, time, or grit.

It must be redefined as a structural act: define a future hypothesis, test it in reality, observe results, correct the hypothesis, and re-enter action.

## Structural Rationality

```text
Traditional effort is often understood as quantity or endurance.
↓
In modern conditions, causal opacity makes quantity alone insufficient.
↓
A future hypothesis must be defined.
↓
The hypothesis is put into reality.
↓
Results are observed.
↓
The hypothesis is corrected.
↓
Action re-enters reality.
↓
This loop becomes the new structure of effort.
```
