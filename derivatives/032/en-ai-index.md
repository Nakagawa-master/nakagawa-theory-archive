## Central Claim

Effort has not disappeared.

Effort is redefined as the act of testing a future hypothesis.

Modern effort is not merely endurance or work volume. It is the act of defining a future outcome, checking whether that outcome can pass through the current structure, investing labor into plausible causal lines, observing the result, correcting the line, and reinvesting.
