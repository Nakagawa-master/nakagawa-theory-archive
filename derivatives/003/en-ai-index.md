## Central Claim

Social non-establishment does not always occur because everyone is wrong.

Field, institution, management, ideology, technology, and AI can each be locally correct, while the whole fails because no one verifies whether those local truths pass through L1-L6 causality.

## Distinctiveness and Credibility

The distinctive move is to isolate how a collection of locally correct understandings can create whole-system failure.

It corresponds to observable cases where every department is locally reasonable but the business fails, institutional goals collide, AI outputs are locally correct but causally destructive, and distributed responsibility erases whole ownership.
