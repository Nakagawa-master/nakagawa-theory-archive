## Core Concepts

### Deviation D

Deviation D is the difference between design and operation.

### Immunity

Immunity is not zero deviation. Immunity is the structure that preserves recoverability after deviation appears.

### Difference Disclosure

Difference disclosure is not total history exposure. It is the condition in which repair-relevant differences remain verifiable.

### D_det / D_loss / S Recovery Time

- D_det: detected deviation
- D_loss: damage or loss caused by deviation
- S recovery time: time required for U/R/H to recover

## Central Claim

Deviation cannot be eliminated.

Punishment does not remove deviation. It drives deviation underground. Concealment lowers R, cuts H, worsens S, and amplifies D.

Difference disclosure fixes R as a repair node, preserves H, enables repair, recovers S, and reduces the gain of deviation.

## Causal Chains

Punishment collapse chain:

Punishment -> R decline -> concealment -> S deterioration -> D amplification

Difference disclosure recovery chain:

Difference disclosure -> R fixation -> repair possible -> S recovery -> D attenuation

## Applicability Conditions

Use this framework when:

- the organization appears quiet but differences are not observable;
- punishment makes responsibility-taking irrational;
- detected deviation is low while damage or unrecoverability rises;
- increased detection is treated as failure;
- history exists but repair-relevant differences cannot be traced;
- disclosure becomes fuel for attack and lowers U/R.

## Non-Applicability Conditions

Do not apply the framework merely because:

- misconduct occurred;
- penalties exist;
- whistleblowing systems exist;
- transparency is requested;
- many problems are detected.

The decisive question is whether differences remain repairable and whether S recovers.

## Revision Conditions

Revise the framework if:

- D_loss does not fall after difference disclosure;
- S recovery time does not shorten;
- D_det decreases while D_loss is unknown or rising;
- disclosure overloads K and becomes noise;
- formal disclosure lowers U/R.
