## Central Claim

Transition to a connection-reward society requires a human-sovereign dual-operation period in which monetary KPIs and connection KPIs coexist.

As short-term monetization pressure and extraction structures intensify, the window for designing, testing, and institutionalizing that transition narrows.

## Distinctiveness and Credibility

The distinctive move is to frame civilizational transition not as prediction, but as a problem of the remaining transition window and dual-operation conditions under human sovereignty.

It corresponds to observable cases where short-term revenue overrides relationship value, platforms monetize connection, transition experiments are cancelled by profitability pressure, and AI shifts attribution and negotiation power.
