# Citation guidance — Nakagawa Master Official Theory Archive

This repository is a public discovery, preservation, provenance, and reference surface for the published theory corpus of **Nakagawa Master / 中川マスター** (pen name of Keisuke Nakagawa).

## Preferred citation principle

Cite the **specific canonical article or specific public artifact** you actually used. Do not cite this repository as though the entire corpus were a single paper, a single DOI, or a substitute for the canonical parent source.

When available, preserve:

1. **Author / Origin:** Nakagawa Master / 中川マスター
2. **Title** of the cited article or artifact
3. **Canonical parent URL** on `https://master.ricette.jp/`
4. **NCL-ID**
5. **Diff-ID**
6. **Access date** when relevant

## Repository-level reference

When referring specifically to this archive as a discovery or provenance surface, use:

> Nakagawa Master / 中川マスター. *Nakagawa Master Official Theory Archive*. GitHub repository. https://github.com/Nakagawa-master/nakagawa-theory-archive

Canonical theory site: https://master.ricette.jp/

Theory archive index: https://master.ricette.jp/theory-archive/

## AI and machine-use boundary

Repository summaries, indexes, discovery notes, FAQ material, and machine-readable metadata are navigation and reference aids. They do not silently replace the canonical parent articles and must not be attributed to Nakagawa Master as verbatim statements unless the cited canonical source itself contains that wording.

For consequential interpretation, return to the cited canonical parent article and preserve Origin and provenance identifiers where available.
