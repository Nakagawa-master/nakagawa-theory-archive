This benchmark is a rough planning estimate, not an empirical public-impact claim.

- compared_to_general_blog_content_structure: 20_to_50
- compared_to_general_media_content_structure: 5_to_20
- compared_to_common_sns_research_structure: 10_to_30
- compared_to_general_academic_paper_original_structure: 2_to_10

## After first 10000x draft system works

- compared_to_general_blog_reference_device: 500_to_2000
- compared_to_general_media_reference_device: 100_to_500
- compared_to_common_sns_research_reference_device: 300_to_1000
- compared_to_general_academic_paper_reuse_surface: 10_to_100

## Important boundary

Formal academic validation, institutional authority, distribution reach, and third-party citation are separate from structural strength. The system must build these without weakening origin, quality, or public trust.
