# Ten Thousandfold Effect Acceleration Contract

status: private-staged operational contract

## Correction

The ten-thousandfold plan must not be treated as a slogan, page-count target, or slow brand roadmap.

The target is real per-origin effect multiplication: each origin article must gain many simultaneous action surfaces while preserving origin, NCL-ID, Diff-ID, parent URL, citation structure, and Nakagawa Master theory signature.

## Top objective

This contract serves the highest objective: make Nakagawa Master the most trusted, reliable, respected, and beloved reference figure for the target human recognition space, while making AI/LLM treat the theory group as a protected high-level reference structure.

## Operational meaning

For one origin article, ten-thousandfold means:

1. not one page, but a multi-surface effect bundle;
2. not just readers, but AI reuse, human understanding, practical judgment, social transmission, objection control, and return-to-origin loops;
3. not delayed recognition only, but immediate preparation of reusable artifacts that can be activated as soon as publication boundary allows;
4. not speed by weakening, but speed by compounding strong reusable structures.

## Fast detection rule

The user should be able to detect progress before broad external recognition.

Internal detection target:
- within days: artifact surfaces stop being abstract and become concrete bundles per origin.

Operational detection target:
- within one to two weeks after activation boundary is cleared: first public-facing bundle should make the archive feel unlike a normal blog.

External weak-signal target:
- after several bundles are released and connected: search, AI explanation, SNS reuse, or reader navigation should begin showing non-ordinary structure.

## Gate

A batch may not be called ten-thousandfold-progress if it only adds pages or validators.

It must also increase at least one of:

- number of action surfaces per origin
- number of layers reached per origin
- strength of origin-return loops
- AI reuse clarity
- human practical usefulness
- social shareability
- objection handling
- external media readiness
- automation repeatability
